# GresIQ

GresIQ is the database service — a managed, PostgreSQL-oriented database for your
app.

## What you can do

- Run a managed PostgreSQL-oriented database
- Configure the service and get connection details
- Reach your data through smbCloud Platform access patterns

## How it fits together

GresIQ is the data layer for your app. You set up the service, grab its
connection details, and connect your app the way you would to any PostgreSQL
database.

## Getting started

1. Create a GresIQ database.
2. Open its connection details.
3. Connect your app and start querying.
