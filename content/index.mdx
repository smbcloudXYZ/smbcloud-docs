# smbCloud Platform

**Four services. One platform.** Enterprise power at startup velocity.

smbCloud is the platform you build your product on. It bundles the pieces most
teams stitch together by hand — shipping apps, signing users in, routing mail,
and running a database — into one place, so you can stay focused on what you're
actually building.
